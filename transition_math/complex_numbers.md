# complex numbers

#### definition

A complex number $z=x+iy$ where $x,y \in \Reals$, $z \in \Complex$

x - real part of $z$
y - imaginary part of $z$

$\Reals \subsetneq \Complex$

$i \in \Complex - \Reals$

#### conjuction

$let: z = x + iy$ then $\bar{z} = x - iy$

#### trygonometric form

$z = |z|(\cos{\alpha} + i\sin{\alpha})$
